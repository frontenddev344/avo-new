Our Story

From a Small Studio
to a Global Force

Founded in 2014 in Mohali, Punjab, Avology Technologies began with a simple mission: deliver world-class digital solutions at an honest price. What started as a 3-person web studio has grown into a 60+ strong team of designers, developers, strategists, and marketers serving clients across 25+ countries.

Over a decade later, we've delivered 10,000+ projects — from startup MVPs to enterprise platforms — always with the same dedication to craft, communication, and results that actually move the needle.
