FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical web project take?
A standard marketing website takes 3–6 weeks. A complex web application with custom features and integrations typically takes 3–6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.
Do you provide hosting and ongoing maintenance?
Yes. We offer managed hosting on AWS or GCP, with 99.9% uptime SLA, automated backups, security patches, and monthly maintenance plans. All projects include 3 months of free post-launch support.
Can you redesign or rebuild my existing website?
Absolutely. We handle full redesigns, partial rebuilds, and migrations from legacy platforms. We preserve SEO equity and ensure zero downtime during the transition.
What is your pricing model?
We offer fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes and time-and-material for ongoing product development. All quotes are transparent with no hidden fees. Contact us for a detailed estimate.
Do you sign NDAs?
Yes, we sign NDAs before any project discussion. Your IP, data, and business ideas are fully protected. We also provide full source code ownership upon project completion.
